About Us


Welcome TIMELESS FILM. We are an independent cinema situated inside a mall in Las Vegas. Unlike other cinemas you know we don't focus so much on showing newly released films rather focus on screening the classics.

We pride ourselves in providing excellent customer service which is something that we continue to strive towards.



‘What are the opening and closing times for the Cinema?’

The cinema is open 10:00-23:00 Monday-Sunday. However, you can access your cinema account 24/7.



‘I am unable to login, what do I do?’

If you have forgotten your password, go to our Contact section, there you will be able to enter your email along with your queary and a member of stuff will send you an email with your new password.



‘First time user wanting to book a film to see, what do I do?’

If you haven't registered an account with us, you must first do that. Having done so, log in using your new usernameID and password, where you will be redirected to a page where you can make a booking.
